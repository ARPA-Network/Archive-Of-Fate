Elderlan, a canvas painted by the gods with stars as brushes and aether as ink, unfolds gently in the river of time. Since the primeval dragons slumber beneath the World's Spine, magic has flowed like breath through all things. Eras turn, and humanity has raised towers and city-states upon ruins. The banners of the Adventurer's Guild flutter in the wind, ancient whispers echo from the depths of dungeons, the light of magic academies illuminates the path of knowledge, knights swear immortal oaths in cathedrals, while ancient dragons still circle above the clouds, and the gods silently watch the clamor of the mortal world. This is an epic age where sword and sorcery intertwine: sorcerers study the paths of the stars, warriors temper the power within their blood, rogues lurk in shadows and secret realms, and the threads of fate coil around every palm. Ancient covenants flow in the blood of nobles, yet the blade of a commoner can slash through predetermined destiny. Wealth, glory, truth, abyss—every road leads to an unknown end. Adventurers gather from all directions, exchanging legends under tavern lights, touching history in the dust of ruins. Their choices not only write personal tales but also sway the fate of the continent. Beneath the skies of Elderlan, hope and despair rise and fall like twin moons; only courage and wisdom can light the way forward.
