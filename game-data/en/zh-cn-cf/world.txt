Above the Nine Heavens, the path of immortality stretches into the void. Since the ancient war of gods and demons, when the celestial pillars broke and the earthly cords snapped, the mortal realm has suffered the calamity of spiritual energy dispersion. Fortunately, the Three Pure Ancestors passed down the five-stage cultivation method: Qi Refining to sense heaven and earth, Foundation Establishment to forge the Dao platform, Golden Core to condense life and soul, Nascent Soul to shed the mortal shell and transform, and finally Divinity Transformation to comprehend the true meaning of the Great Dao, allowing one to break through the void and ascend. For millions of years, mortals have defied heaven and fought for their fate, seeking a thread of longevity amidst the mundane world. Sects stand in myriad, secret realms hide treasures; at tribulation crossing, heavenly thunder tempers the body, while inner demons can sink one in a single thought. In this world, lifespan can be extended for centuries, yet karma is like a net, with blessings and misfortunes intertwined. Dao companions may share profound mysteries, yet they can also become the shackles of love tribulations. Truly: Who stands the highest at the end of the immortal road? Into the Nine Heavens, a thousand bones decay. When the wind rises, a youth with a sword enters the mountains; deep in the clouds, a crane cries through the nine heavens, and another cycle of fate unfolds.
